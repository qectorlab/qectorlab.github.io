About

QECTOR Decoder v3

A source-available Rust/Python platform for high-performance quantum error-correction decoding, built with a focus on reproducibility, decoder breadth, and commercial usability.

Project Philosophy

QECTOR was created to solve a practical problem: researchers and commercial teams need more than just one fast MWPM decoder. They need a reliable, reproducible platform that supports multiple decoding strategies, LDPC/qLDPC workflows, GPU acceleration, and clear commercial licensing terms.

The project prioritizes measurable results over marketing claims, full artifact reproducibility, and honest positioning of where QECTOR adds value versus free tools like PyMatching and Stim.
