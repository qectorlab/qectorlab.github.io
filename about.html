About

QECTOR Lab

Building practical, high-performance tools for quantum error correction research and evaluation.

QECTOR Lab is an independent research and development effort focused on creating production-grade quantum error correction software. The project emphasizes reproducibility, clean architecture, and real-world usability for both academic researchers and commercial teams exploring fault-tolerant quantum computing.

Core Principles

  • Reproducible results with full artifacts and code
  • Clean, modular architecture (Rust core + Python surface)
  • Practical tooling that works alongside existing QEC ecosystems
  • Transparent licensing and clear commercial paths
