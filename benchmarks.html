Show the data, scope the claim.

GPU batch proof

The claim boundary remains strict

Allowed today

CUDA/OpenCL batch paths are tested for CPU bit-identity on supported configurations. This is a correctness/fidelity claim.

Not allowed yet

No public 2× GPU, RTX superiority, SaaS readiness, OEM readiness, or real-time hardware decoding claim without a reproducible local artifact campaign.

Needed proof

Head-to-head batch throughput runs against PyMatching + Stim with hardware, driver, batch size, circuit, seed, wall time, memory, and hashes.
