Claim boundary

The boundary remains strict.

Allowed today

Scoped evidence, benchmark artifacts, parity on tested workloads, and selected accuracy-mode results.

Not allowed yet

No universal speed claim, no production SaaS claim, no OEM readiness claim, and no real-time hardware decoding claim by default.

Needed proof

Hardware-specific runs need circuit, seed, driver, package version, wall time, memory, output files, and hashes.

Source-available QEC decoding for reproducible research and commercial workflow evaluation.
