Commercial Positioning

QECTOR is for R&D teams comparing decoder workflows

Strong fit

Labs and startups that need a single source-available platform for Union-Find, Blossom, Sparse Blossom, BP-OSD, streaming, batch decoding, GPU batch paths, and reproducible benchmark artifacts.

Weak fit if you only need fastest MWPM.
If the only requirement is surface-code exact MWPM latency, free open-source stacks are the benchmark to beat. QECTOR's commercial value must come from workflow breadth, packaging, BP-OSD/LDPC support, GPU batch workflows, and legal clarity.
