Executive Summary

QECTOR Decoder v3 is a quantum error correction decoder suite with a high-performance Rust core (Union-Find, exact polynomial MWPM/Blossom, Sparse Blossom, BP-OSD) and a pure-Python ecosystem layer for Stim, PyMatching, and Sinter compatibility.

Accuracy
Belief-matching achieves lower logical error rates than PyMatching on real Stim circuit-level noise (-33.7% at d=5, -25.7% at d=7).
Parity
QECTOR weighted exact MWPM matches PyMatching LER at every distance d=3 to d=15 with correct threshold scaling.
Correctness
832 / 832 tests passing. GPU batch decoders are bit-identical to CPU. Full reproducibility with SHA-256 artifacts.
Ecosystem
Native Stim DEM loader, drop-in PyMatching API, and Sinter plug-in for standardized Monte-Carlo benchmarking.

Honest Limitations

PyMatching remains the latency leader (typically 3x–24x faster depending on distance). Belief-matching is the accuracy mode and is slower per shot. BP-OSD is competitive but trails the specialized 'ldpc' package by ~10%. Union-Find is a fast approximate path.
