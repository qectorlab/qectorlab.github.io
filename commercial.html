Why license QECTOR?

Why pay when PyMatching and Stim are free?

Baseline honesty: PyMatching remains the latency leader for exact MWPM and Stim remains the simulator baseline. QECTOR should not be sold as a universal replacement for either.

Commercial value

QECTOR packages multiple decoder families, BP-OSD/LDPC workflows, selected belief-matching accuracy experiments, CPU/GPU batch validation, source access, reproducibility artifacts, and commercial legal permission in one platform.
