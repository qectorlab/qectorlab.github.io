What Happens Next

After you send a message

Step 1

Acknowledgement

You'll receive an acknowledgement email within 1 business day confirming your message was received and identifying who will respond and in what timeframe.

Step 2

Scoping response

For license inquiries, the initial response outlines the applicable tier, proposed terms, and any clarifying questions needed to draft the license proposal. For technical questions, you receive a direct answer or a follow-up request for specifics.

Step 3

License proposal or resolution

For commercial licenses, a formal proposal with draft license terms and an invoice is sent within 5 business days of the scoping exchange. Technical questions are typically resolved within the initial response thread.

No sales team, no CRM, no automated sequences. All messages go directly to Guillaume Lessard. You will get a real response from a person who knows the codebase.
