Decoding is the critical bottleneck

As quantum processors scale toward fault tolerance, the classical decoding layer becomes one of the most severe constraints. Slow or inaccurate decoders force hardware teams to over-provision physical qubits, inflate cryogenic infrastructure, and delay the path to utility-scale systems.

One framework. Multiple algorithms.

Modern fault-tolerant systems cannot rely on a single decoding heuristic. QECTOR provides a unified, production-grade platform with interchangeable algorithms so teams can dynamically trade accuracy for latency based on real-time syndrome density and hardware constraints.

Blossom MWPM
Exact weighted matching with adaptive-k optimization for near-exact quality at scale.
Belief-Matching
BP + reweighted MWPM for correlated errors. Lower LER than standard MWPM.
BP-OSD
Competitive on qLDPC codes where matching fundamentally fails.
Union-Find
Ultra-fast approximate path for latency-critical workloads.
