Licensing Overview

QECTOR is source-available with clear paths for both non-commercial and commercial use.

The project follows a dual licensing model: a permissive path for personal/academic use and structured commercial licensing for startups, labs, and enterprises.

Key Points

  • Personal and academic use is free
  • Commercial use requires a paid license
  • Full SATI OS platform has separate tiered pricing
  • Source code is available for inspection under license terms

Quantum error correction decoder package for reproducible research and commercial workflow evaluation.
