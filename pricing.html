Commercial Pricing

Market-grounded QECTOR pricing

QECTOR is free for personal, academic, educational, and non-commercial research use. Commercial pricing is now aligned with the current product reality: a promising v0.4 Rust/Python QEC decoder platform for multi-decoder R&D, GPU batch workflows, BP-OSD/LDPC experimentation, and commercial legal clarity. It is not priced as a mature real-time hardware QEC stack.

What you are paying for

Defendable value

QECTOR is sold as a commercial R&D package: decoder diversity, reproducible benchmark artifacts, BP-OSD/LDPC paths, CPU/GPU batch workflows, source access, and legal permission for commercial use.

Not a fastest MWPM claim.
PyMatching and Stim remain powerful free alternatives. QECTOR pricing must clear the build-versus-buy threshold for teams that need more than a free MWPM decoder.
