What commercial licenses include

Depending on the agreement tier

Use rights

Written authorization to use QECTOR Decoder for the permitted commercial purposes defined in your agreement — product, SaaS, internal, OEM, or enterprise.

License document

A formal signed license document identifying your organization, the permitted use, the license term, and the pricing. Required before any commercial use begins.

Support

Email-based support for integration questions, bug reports, and technical guidance. Priority support channels available on Professional tier and above.

Integration guidance

Technical guidance on integrating QECTOR into your workflow, including GPU backend selection, benchmark interpretation, and Stim/Sinter compatibility.

Roadmap access

Enterprise and strategic tiers receive access to the private QECTOR development roadmap, including planned features for QECTOR Workbench and decoder extensions.

NDA available

Non-disclosure agreements are available for Strategic and OEM tier discussions. Enterprise NDA available on request prior to commercial negotiations.
