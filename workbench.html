QECTOR Workbench

Local Fullstack QEC Benchmarking Application

Load circuits, compare decoders, export reproducible artifacts, and generate professional technical reports — all from one local desktop application.

Join Early Access
Key Capabilities

Designed for serious QEC research and commercial workflows

Circuit & DEM Loading

Load .stim circuits and detector error models directly with full validation and parsing.

Multi-Decoder Comparison

Run head-to-head comparisons between QECTOR, PyMatching, and other decoders with real data.

Reproducible Artifacts

Export PDF reports, CSV/JSON results with SHA-256 hashes, git commit stamps, and full environment snapshots.

Backend Diagnostics

Real-time CPU, CUDA, and OpenCL status with automatic backend selection and calibration.

Visual Analytics

Generate LER curves, latency percentiles, threshold plots, and comparison charts from real benchmark data.

Job Management

FIFO job queue with cancel support, live progress tracking, and batch processing capabilities.

Target Users

Built for professionals who need reproducible results

Quantum Research Labs

Researchers who need to run controlled decoder comparisons and produce publication-ready benchmark artifacts with full reproducibility.

Commercial QEC Teams

Teams evaluating multiple decoders for product integration who require documented, auditable benchmark results.

Hardware Developers

Teams building quantum control systems or ASICs who need to validate decoder performance on target hardware.

Academic Groups

Universities and research groups that need reproducible workflows for papers and grant reporting.
